Key Concepts

Tiling: Windows auto-arrange to fill the screen. Use split (V/B) before opening new windows to control layout direction.
Floating: Mod+Shift+Space makes a window free-floating (drag with Mod+mouse if needed).
Scratchpad: Mod+Shift+Minus stashes a window, Mod+Minus shows it (toggle).
Stacking/Tabbed: Mod+S for stacking layout, Mod+W for tabbed, Mod+E for split.

Neovim (LazyVim)

leader = Space — modal editing — LSP + Telescope + Treesitter

Tip: Press <leader> (Space) and wait to see all available keybinds via which-key. Your keyboard's NAV layer (hold Space for arrows) works in insert mode; in normal mode, just use h/j/k/l directly.

Vim Grammar Pattern

verb + noun: d(delete) + w(word) = delete word
Common verbs: d(delete) c(change) y(yank/copy) v(visual select)
Common nouns: w(word) b(back word) $(end of line) 0(start of line) G(end of file) gg(start of file)
Text objects: iw(inner word) i"(inner quotes) i((inner parens) ip(inner paragraph) it(inner tag)
Multiplier: 3dw = delete 3 words, 5j = move down 5 lines
